Behind The Chair

Meet The Team

Ilhan Okur

Owner & Master Barber

Ilhan opened Istanbul Barbershop with a simple idea — bring the warmth and craft of a traditional Turkish barber shop to the people of Ramsey. With years of experience behind the chair and an eye for detail honed over countless cuts, every visit feels less like a quick trim and more like time well spent.

Whether it's a sharp skin fade, a classic short-back-and-sides or a hot-towel beard trim, Ilhan takes pride in getting it right and making every customer feel at home. Walk in for a cut, leave looking and feeling your best.
